Accessibility Testing


Why should Top Quality Consulting conduct your accessibility testing?

Expertise in Accessibility: Top Quality Consulting specializes in accessibility testing, understanding the principles and best practices of accessibility compliance. We remain updated on the latest accessibility standards, guidelines, and regulations to ensure that your software caters to users with disabilities effectively.

Comprehensive Testing Approach: Top Quality Consulting conducts thorough accessibility testing across various aspects of your software, including user interface (UI), navigation, content, multimedia, forms, keyboard accessibility, screen reader compatibility, and more. We evaluate your software against accessibility standards like Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) and Section 508, providing a comprehensive assessment of its accessibility.

Compliance and Legal Requirements: Top Quality Consulting assists in ensuring that your software meets accessibility compliance requirements, including legal obligations such as the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA). Through our accessibility testing, we pinpoint potential barriers and help you address them, mitigating legal risks and ensuring inclusivity for all users.

User-Centric Approach: When conducting accessibility testing, Top Quality Consulting prioritizes user experience and usability. We empathize with the challenges faced by users with disabilities and strive to deliver an inclusive and user-friendly experience. By evaluating accessibility features and conducting user testing with individuals with disabilities, we help you create a software product that is accessible, intuitive, and usable for a diverse user base.

Early Issue Identification: Our accessibility testing methodology focuses on early issue identification within the development cycle. By integrating accessibility testing early on, you can proactively address issues, reducing time and effort needed for fixing problems in later stages. Early identification and resolution of issues lead to cost savings, streamlined development processes, and a more accessible end product.

Remediation Guidance and Recommendations: Top Quality Consulting offers actionable recommendations and guidance based on the results of accessibility testing. We provide insights into identified accessibility issues and offer advice on effective solutions. This guidance equips you with a clear roadmap for enhancing your software's accessibility, empowering you to make necessary improvements.

Ensuring accessibility through testing enables people with disabilities to navigate and interact effectively with website or application content and features. Making your website accessible not only expands your potential audience but also minimizes legal risks while showcasing social responsibility. Furthermore, designing for accessibility often enhances overall user experience regardless of disability.
